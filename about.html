About KFC Pakistan

Our journey to bringing Finger Lickin' Good chicken to you.

Our Story: A Taste of Legacy

KFC's journey began with Colonel Harland Sanders, an entrepreneur who started selling fried chicken from his roadside restaurant in Corbin, Kentucky, during the Great Depression. He perfected his secret recipe of 11 herbs and spices, a recipe that remains a closely guarded secret to this day.

In Pakistan, KFC opened its first restaurant in Karachi in 1997. Since then, we've grown to become one of the most beloved fast-food chains, serving millions of happy customers across the country. Our commitment to quality, taste, and exceptional customer service has made us a household name.

We pride ourselves on using locally sourced, fresh chicken and maintaining the highest standards of hygiene and food preparation. Every piece of chicken is hand-breaded and cooked to golden perfection, ensuring that authentic Finger Lickin' Good taste in every bite.
